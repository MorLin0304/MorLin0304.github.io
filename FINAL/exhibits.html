Exhibits

Modern

In the West Wing is the Modern Exhibit, here you will find information on Modern (2000 and on) day clown practices and learn about the current day contemporaries.

Historical

In the East Wing is the Historical Exhibit, here you will find information about the colorful history of Clownery as well as preserved artifacts such as props and costumes dated back centuries.

Interactive

Interactive events switch out every month and take place in the South Wing of the museum. It is included with your Upgraded ticket or $15 to participate. No prior experience is needed and it is for all ages 6 and up.
