FAQ

Is photography allowed?

Yes! Photography is allowed as long as flash is off and you are a proper distance away from the exhibits. We also ask that if you photograph the Interactive Event, you please only photograph you, your party, and the instructor as other patrons may not want to be photographed.

Do you offer tours?

Yes, there are free tours going on periodically free of charge. You may ask the front desk for the times and where to wait.

Are you open on Holidays?

We are open on all holidays however our hours will change. You may check the site to see the updated hours when the holidays roll around.

You may contact us for any other questions!
